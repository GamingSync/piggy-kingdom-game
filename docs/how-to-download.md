The Piggy Kingdom Game is an engaging and strategic adventure where players build, explore, and conquer a whimsical world. Follow the steps below to download and set up the game.

## Option 1 — Browser / Web App

### Step 1 — Access the Game Website
1. Open your preferred web browser.
2. Go to the official Piggy Kingdom Game website.

### Step 2 — Create an Account
1. Click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button.
2. Fill in the required fields, including your email and password.
3. Confirm your email address through the verification link sent to your inbox.

### Step 3 — Start Playing
1. Log in to your account on the Piggy Kingdom Game website.
2. Click on "Play Now" to begin your adventure.

---

## Option 2 — Desktop App

### Step 1 — Download the App
1. Visit the Piggy Kingdom Game website.
2. Navigate to the "Download" section.
3. Choose the version compatible with your operating system (Windows or macOS).

### Step 2 — Install the App
1. Open the downloaded file.
2. Follow the installation prompts to complete the setup.
3. Launch the Piggy Kingdom Game app after installation.
